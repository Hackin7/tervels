Doge Palace

There's the governance place, Armoury and prison

We were rushing through it a fair bit because we got delayed from Museum Correr but it was actually very nice, what stunned me was that the paintings filled the entire walls, and there were a lot of them

In the governance areas you get to see the Senate, voting area, confession areas.
You also get to see the rehabilitation areas, and it's nice to see the contrast in the paintings between the fighting war art and the rehab art

There was meaning and reason for the art in the room which was really really nice

Vastly underrated, glad I chose this over Saint Mark's Basilica (cheaper too!), can go that another time maybe

The outside of Saint Mark's Basilica was quite nice, it's very ex to go inside though
Oh well another time
