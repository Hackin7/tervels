# Mall

My friend suggested we go to the mall for the toilets

I am not disappointed

This mall has vibes (and Singapore stuff)

The top floor is good, got things to see

The gift shop is quite cute lmao

So my friend and I decided to go into the museum, which was highly recommended

There were 3 main exhibits

The 1st one was kinda weird, you connect to a phone game， have an avatar show up on a screen and it follows you along, with audio guides, collectibles etc. 

But it all made sense when I saw the painting, you are kinda living the old painting lol. I thought that concept was quite cool

Lol gamification

The 2nd exhibition was really on flower paintings, a lot of them in a lot of different styles. 

Ye it was kinda overwhelming, but it was nice, I took too many photos to put them all up, the pieces are from all sorts of east west places

The last exhibition was on Beeple, I think is some digital art thing, honestly the kind of thing I vibe with (having done 3D animation stuff myself)

Quite cool and meme lmao

The rest of the mall was more standard, welp
