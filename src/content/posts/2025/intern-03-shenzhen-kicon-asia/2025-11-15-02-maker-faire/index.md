# Maker Faire

### Day 1

Too much at Maker Faire Shenzhen to condense into images but I think it's quite nice

1. The maker projects section had really cool projects by schools, individual makers, makers all over the globe
2. The companies that were there were well known, like Raspberry Pi, Seeed, Clockwork Pi, etc.
3. Some interactive booths, soldering

It's what I expect a Maker Faire to be. 

But also it really feels similar to Maker Faire Singapore back in the day, it's quite nice, but not too out of field that SG didn't do before, which is nice
